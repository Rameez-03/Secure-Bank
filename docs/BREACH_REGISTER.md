# Personal Data Breach Register — Secure Bank

**Document type:** Mandatory record under Article 33(5) UK GDPR  
**Owner:** Rameez (data controller)  
**Purpose:** All personal data breaches — whether reported to the ICO or not — must be recorded here. This register is an internal document and is not published.

---

## ICO Notification Reference

ICO Report a Breach portal: https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/report-a-breach/personal-data-breach/  
ICO helpline: 0303 123 1113

**Notification obligation:** Article 33 UK GDPR requires notification to the ICO within **72 hours** of becoming aware of a breach that is likely to result in a risk to individuals' rights and freedoms. Breaches unlikely to result in such risk must still be documented here but do not require ICO notification.

---

## Breach Response Procedure

On becoming aware of a potential breach:

| Step | Action | Owner | Timeframe |
|------|--------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Contain — stop the breach progressing (e.g. revoke credentials, take endpoint offline) | Developer | Immediately |
| 2 | Assess — determine what data was affected, how many individuals, likely risk level | Developer | Within 24 hrs |
| 3 | Decide — determine if ICO notification is required (risk to individuals' rights and freedoms) | Developer | Within 48 hrs |
| 4 | Notify ICO — if required, report via portal above with: nature of breach, categories/volume of data, likely consequences, measures taken | Developer | Within 72 hrs of awareness |
| 5 | Notify data subjects — if high risk to individuals, notify affected users without undue delay | Developer | ASAP after ICO notification |
| 6 | Document — record in this register regardless of whether ICO was notified | Developer | Within 72 hrs |
| 7 | Remediate — identify and fix root cause; update SECURITY.md | Developer | As soon as practical |

## Risk Classification Guide

| Risk Level | Description | ICO Notification Required |
|-----------|-------------|--------------------------|
| Low | Minimal impact; data not particularly sensitive; small number of individuals; unlikely any harm | No — document only |
| Medium | Some personal data exposed; limited number of individuals; unlikely significant harm | Possibly — assess case by case |
| High | Sensitive personal data (financial, health, etc.); multiple individuals; potential for discrimination, financial loss, or reputational harm | Yes |
| Critical | Large-scale breach; particularly sensitive data; likely significant harm to individuals | Yes — and notify data subjects |
